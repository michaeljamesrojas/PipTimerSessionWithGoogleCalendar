Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how PiP Timer ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web application.

Information We Collect

We collect and use the following information only when you explicitly grant permission:

  • Google Calendar access to create and save timer sessions as calendar events
  • Basic Google profile information to identify your calendar

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Creating calendar events for your timer sessions
  • Customizing event titles and colors as per your preferences

Data Storage

PiP Timer does not store any of your data on our servers. All timer sessions are saved directly to your Google Calendar, and any customization preferences are stored locally in your browser.

Google Calendar API

We use Google Calendar API with the following permissions:

  • Create events in your calendar
  • View your basic profile information

We only request the minimum permissions necessary for the app to function.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Using HTTPS for all data transfers
  • Following OAuth 2.0 security practices for Google Calendar integration
  • Not storing any sensitive information on our servers

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
